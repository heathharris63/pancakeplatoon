P-Platoon

The Crepe Cavalry

The Crepe Calvery is a select group of skills players (skinny boys) that have earned their way into the platoon by making defenders feel cheap. They have earned their spot by making a pancake on a block, and depending on the nastyness and amount of their pancakes they earn their ranks.











Noah Holsinger

First Lieutenant, Noah Holsinger, has played many positions over his career at the Trinity Christian Academy Lions. He started out as a Slot Reciever and back up kicker for his first two seasons. Then in his junior year he moved to the role of starting kicker, Slot WR, subbed at Running Back, and played many defensive roles. During his senior year if he was not running the ball on offense, he was pancaking a defender, and still managed to kick many field goals, including one a 54 yarder. He is the highest ranked member of the Crepe Calvery.

Zach Yarbrough

Corporal Zach Yarbrough was a defensive specialist for the first two years of his career. His junior and senior years he played Tightend and fullback on the offensive side of the ball as well.

Lane Lamberth

Sergent Lane Lamberth has been a wide receiver for the passed three years of his career, and is about to entire his fourth. Lane has pancaked many corners on blocking assignments, and still managed to be one of the most iffluencian recievers on the team.
