Coach Mancini

The General: Leads with an Iron fist. Coach Mancini started his football career at Collierville Highschool in Menphis Tennessee. He was a three year starter and first team All Region. He then accepted a full scholorship to the University of Tennessee at Martin. At martin he played as a true freshman and started the next three years.His biggest games were against Tennessee, Memphis, and Auburn. In 2011 he was selected as second team All American. In the same year he achieved first team all conference for the Ohio Valley conference. In his time at Martin he only gave up two sacks in four years. After an impressive college run, he began his coaching career at Obion County in West Tennessee. He coached the offensive and defensive line. In 2015 he took a job as the offensive cordinator at Trinity Christian Academy in Jackson. He also coaches the offensive and defensive line. He still coaches at Trinity Today.
